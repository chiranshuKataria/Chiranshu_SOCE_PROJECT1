Me

Mr. Chiranshu Kataria is currently pursuing his undergraduate studies at the esteemed Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur , where he is in his first year of academic pursuit. Hailing from Gurugram, Haryana. Alongside his academic endeavours, Mr. Kataria holds multiple notable positions within the university. Additionally, he holds the esteemed position of the President's Secretary, a testament to his leadership skills and his ability to efficiently manage administrative responsibilities.

His involvement in this team showcases his dedication and passion for innovative technological advancements. Outside of his academic and professional pursuits, Mr. Kataria harbours a keen interest in videography, demonstrating his skill and artistry in capturing visual moments. He also finds solace in the melodic world of music and enjoys engaging in the creative process of writing emails. Moreover, Mr. Kataria 's passions extend beyond these domains as he exhibits a strong inclination towards video editing, animation, and consulting, exemplifying his diverse skill set and inclination for versatile ventures. Additionally, he expresses a genuine curiosity in exploring the realm of web development, desiring to expand his knowledge and expertise in this field.
